Who created it?

Fireboy and Watergirl is a puzzle-platform video game series created by indie game developer Oslo Albet.

When was it created?

The first game of the series, Fireboy and Watergirl in the Forest Temple was released on November 19, 2009.

Where can you play this game?

You can play this game on many different websites like Cool Math Games.com, Crazy Games.com, Agame.com, etc. You can even play it on Android devices as well.
